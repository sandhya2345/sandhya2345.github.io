1

Creating a pet adoption website using HTML, CSS, and Node.js involves building a platform where users can view, filter, and inquire about pets available for adoption. The homepage typically introduces the website with featured pets, statistics, and testimonials to engage visitors. A comprehensive pet listings page allows users to browse through available pets, with filters for categories like type, age, and breed to streamline the search process. Each pet has a dedicated details page, providing extensive information, images, and adoption procedures.
